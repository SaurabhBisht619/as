ABOUT US

NEPAL EMERGENCY COVID FUND(NECF)  ​    
is a vibrant movement made up of people who are committed to improving the health and well-being of the most undeserved people of Nepal. It is a nonpartisan, apolitical organization that works in partnership with local community-based organizations to provide health care services through the integrated health approach. NECF is registered  as a non-profit organization in Nepal. We operate through our office based in Kathmandu, Nepal.  ​​
 
OUR HISTORY 
In early 2020, a group of young health professionals from Nepal with interest in health and development of Nepal  came together to discuss about ways they could give back to the country and the communities. It was natural that they decided to do so by providing clinical care and implementing public health programs in the communities.
 
OUR MISSION 
NECF advocates for public health policies and programs that promote the health and well-being  of the underserved  people in rural and urban areas of Nepal to realize their optimum potential health.
To advance our mission we work with many partners to:
 - Provide financial support and technical assistance to community-based organizations
- Conduct public health research
- Provide direct clinical care through comprehensive primary health care approach

OUR APPROACH
We work on following focal areas that are important to health
- Basic physical and mental health services
- Safe water and sanitation
- Maternal and child health services
- Food access
- Health   education
 - Health technology and informatics
